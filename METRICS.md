# Metrics

## North Star Metric

Completed AI audits per week.

This directly measures whether users find value in the product.

---

## Supporting Metrics

### 1. Audit Completion Rate

Percentage of users who complete the audit flow.

### 2. Email Capture Rate

Percentage of users submitting reports after viewing savings.

### 3. Returning Users

Measures long-term usefulness and retention.

---

## Important Early Signals

- users sharing audit results
- repeated usage
- strong email conversion
- consultation interest

---

## Pivot Decision

If audit completion is high but email conversion stays extremely low, the value proposition or trust factor would need improvement.
