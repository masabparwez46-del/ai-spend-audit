# Go To Market Strategy

## Target Users

The ideal users are:

- startup founders
- indie hackers
- small engineering teams
- AI-heavy startups

These users often subscribe to multiple AI tools without properly tracking costs.

---

## User Pain Point

Most teams do not realize:

- how much they are overspending
- which plans are unnecessary
- where cheaper alternatives exist

The tool simplifies this analysis instantly.

---

## Distribution Strategy

Initial growth channels:

- Twitter/X founder community
- Indie Hackers
- Reddit startup communities
- LinkedIn posts
- Hacker News

---

## First 100 Users

I would focus on:

- posting demo videos
- sharing savings examples
- founder communities
- cold outreach to startups

The goal would be collecting emails and validating demand quickly.
